Headteacher's Welcome

Brynhyfryd is a bilingual co-educational comprehensive school for pupils between 11 and 18 years of age, maintained by the Denbighshire Education Authority. Our bilingual ethos reflects the vibrant community we proudly serve.

At Brynhyfryd, we are committed to providing an education of the highest standard that meets the individual needs of every pupil within a supportive and happy bilingual environment. We encourage every student to develop self-respect and maintain a positive, responsible attitude towards life.
