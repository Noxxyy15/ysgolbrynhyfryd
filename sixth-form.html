Our Sixth Form Community

Here at Ysgol Brynhyfryd's Canolfan y Chweched, we aim to create a purposeful and supportive environment that gives all our students the best possible chance to achieve success.

The Sixth Form Centre provides a high quality education with enrichment opportunities for all, with a welcoming and vibrant community, which allows our students to thrive.

We are committed to encouraging the full potential of all our students so they can leave us as well-rounded individuals, who are prepared for the exciting opportunities that present themselves beyond A-Levels.

The Sixth Form Centre actively promotes positive attitudes in our community, which will be evident in our students' approach to their studies, and the wider life of the school.
